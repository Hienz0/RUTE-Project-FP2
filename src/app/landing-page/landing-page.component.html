About Us

RUTE is a comprehensive web-based tourism platform tailored to meet the needs of the local tourism ecosystem in Ubud, Bali. Our platform empowers local tourism service providers and bridges the gap between them and global tourists.

We offer centralized booking for accommodation, tour guides, restaurants, and transportation services. Our goal is to enhance visibility and accessibility for local offerings while promoting sustainable tourism practices.

At RUTE, we are committed to creating a sense of community and collaboration among local stakeholders. We empower them to thrive in the digital era while preserving Ubud's cultural and environmental heritage.
